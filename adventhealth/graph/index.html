Pipeline

How the evidence model builds the review

Current source mode: http / graph_wide_candidate_bullets.

01

Source records

Life Graph supplies durable career records: experience, projects, skills, education, language, source links, normalized facts, and curated claims.

02

Requirement atoms

The job posting is normalized into requirement rows with required/preferred status, requirement kind, priority, and coverage target.

03

Candidate evidence pool

The workflow gathers evidence-backed claims and links them to source objects, skills, projects, and public or sensitive proof context.

04

Evidence scoring

Each requirement keeps the best supporting evidence and exposes component scores, status, support candidates, and recommended next action.

05

Presentation layer

The generated artifacts are rendered as native Next pages: overview, resume, evidence matrix, methodology, selected projects, and evidence model.

How to read the evidence

Claims are not just prose. They are linked back to source objects, skills, projects, and proof context.

Private corporate evidence can support a claim without publishing private files or PHI-bearing artifacts.
